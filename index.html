Feeding Guide & Tips

TAILIVA Feeding Instructions

Serving Chart (Daily Amounts):

Up to 2 kg (Small Cat): 70–100 g
2–4 kg (Medium Cat): 100–150 g
4–6 kg (Large Cat): 150–200 g
Above 6 kg (Extra Large): 200–250 g

Feeding Tips:

  • Divide daily serving into 2–3 meals for better digestion
  • Always provide fresh water alongside meals
  • Adjust portions for very active, kitten, or older cats
  • Thaw frozen portions in refrigerator before serving
  • Consult your vet for personalized feeding advice
